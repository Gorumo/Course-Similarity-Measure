Abstract

The ontology contains a description of the Mass Open Online Course (MOOC). The interrelations between the curriculum developers, teachers, organizers, learners, educational programs, competences and subject areas are considered. The description of the attributes of the MOOC is given. The ontology does not include a description of the internal structure of the course.
